# Make-sports-viewing-affordable-again
fostv.biz to pressure networks to end subscriptions
Hello American Sports Fans!
Remember after a long 5 day workweek you could just turn your TV on and watch to whatever sporting event is on whether it was baseball, football, hockey, basketball, soccer, tennis, etc.? All you needed was a cable subscription and a TV guide to find what you wanted to watch in the comfort of your own home.
Then, in 2025, your cable supplier started hitting you with “If you want to watch THIS game, YOU need a subscription to network X. Add to that, they offer you a 30 day subscription that when the 30 days is up, the subscription CONTINUES until YOU cancel it.
I looked up the schedule for my home NFL team in 2026 and estimated that it will cost over $400 to watch all 16 games at home!! 
Now the networks have pulled extra millions out of sports fan’s pockets for every sporting event in the past year. My question to you is “Do you think that this viewing tax will ever stop or become less expensive? We all know the answer to that question.
I started (fostv.biz) Free Our Sports TV.biz to organize a block of sports fans to protest this new scheme by the networks. Please look at the site, sign the petition and even send a video with your feelings and suggestions. You can buy bumper stickers too. All may join for free and are encouraged to participate however you can.
We need to organize our substantial numbers and push back on this fleecing scheme by the networks. They need to know that there are millions of fans who can boycott any of their advertisers. Support this site and send them a message that we will not accept their tax on our sports viewing.
